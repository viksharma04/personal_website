About Me

Learning new things and pursuing positive change motivates me. I grew up with two small business entrepreneurs for parents and have seen the magical effects of passion and determination. Jean-Baptiste Say, a French economist who first coined the word entrepreneur in about 1800, said: “The entrepreneur shifts economic resources out of an area of lower and into an area of higher productivity and greater yield.” I believe each of us is given the opportunity to align ourselves with a purpose – my purpose is innovation and efficiency.

I am passionate about working with teams to achieve goals, work on problems, derive solutions, and getting the ball rolling. I aim to develop a skill set in project & team management, data science and analysis, financial modelling, business model development, and problem solving. I hope to use these skills to help organizations and individuals achieve goals that align with my purpose of innovation and efficiency. There is a multitude of different opportunities to utilize economic resources to build a better world for each of us. I hope to be a part of as many of these opportunities as possible.
